AWN Smart Farms extends the AgWeatherNet ecosystem into precision agriculture, providing growers with a dedicated platform for monitoring and managing distributed IoT sensor networks deployed across their fields. The platform visualizes real-time data streams from field sensors — including soil moisture, temperature, and microclimate conditions — enabling growers to make precise, timely decisions about irrigation, crop health, and resource management.

I contributed to the frontend architecture of the Smart Farms platform as part of the broader AgWeatherNet web modernization effort, building responsive data visualization components and integrating live IoT data feeds into an intuitive dashboard interface.
